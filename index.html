What is AI Guide?

AI Guide helps users explore the AI landscape by organizing tools into 8 sectors. Each sector highlights the most popular applications, updated over time, with simple explanations for each tool.

How It Works

  1. Browse a sector
  2. Tap an AI icon
  3. Read a clear explanation
